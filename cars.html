Data Sheet Translation

Discovering modern equals of parts for Y32 chassis Cedric/Gloria has been difficult due to little English information online. Below is a database for everything confirmed by Os Ard.

Note:

  1. Fog lamp original bulb may have bullet connector and will need conversion to spade type.
  2. Some parts are shared across Nissan platforms:
    1. Same as RB25DET (R33)
    2. Same as VG30DETT (Z32)
  3. Cedric had "brick" style headlamps with parking lights whereas Gloria had "round" style headlights that integrate the turn signal. There are some differences in the bulb sizes due to split of hi/lo beams and parking lights being in the fog lamp assembly for the Gloria.
