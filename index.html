Os Ard is Irish for "Out Loud" and captures our core design principle to be noticed and create discomfort. We aspire toward aesthetic choices that innovate while being unacceptable to the accessible. The surprise of the auto form.

Static suspension at extreme low ride heights, excessive camber, and lip to fender mastery are only some of our work. We capture unique exhaust tones and body details to turns heads and be asked: how.

The discomfort of the drive is success. There can be no style without suffering.

Os Ardはアイルランド語で「大声で」を意味し、リップからフェンダーまでの熟練の技と、近寄りがたい人には受け入れられない革新的な美的選択に重点を置くという、私たちの核となるデザイン原則を要約しています。 オートフォルムの驚き。
